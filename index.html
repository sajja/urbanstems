About Urban Stems

We at Urban Stems believe that the best way to say it is with flowers!
We work hard and have fun, meeting interesting people and most importantly get to be creative through interesting briefs, beautiful venues, fantastic clients and of course wonderful, colorful flowers The concept at Urban Stems is simple. We will provide strong design with high quality flowers, delivered and managed with exceptional service.
Allow us to bring this world to life for you.

Flower arrangements

Enhancing the beauty of flowers with artistic arrangement is the art of flower arrangements. Flower arrangements are an important part of the ambiance on any occasion, weddings, birthdays, parties or any other events in life.

Weddings

Each bride has a vision for her wedding day. Our mission is to ensure that vision be perfectly manifested. Surround your guest in a floral wonderland with beautiful centerpieces and decorations by offering fresh or silk flowers to add something special for any celebration. We offer unique packages to match your personality and budget

Parties and event flowers

We decorate great events and parties, large and small. We are renowned for our attention to detail, personal approach, can do attitude and calm manner

Houseplants

Houseplants make a beautiful, and inexpensive, decorative accent, adding color and warmth to virtually any room in your home or apartment. Either you are an expert or a forgetful gardner you will love our collection of great indoor plants that will suite you.
